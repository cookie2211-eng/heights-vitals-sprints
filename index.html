1 · Assessment framework

How to read creative performance
Days 1–14
Early signal
Monitor CPAs and spend allocation. Strong performance here = potential unicorn. Act — don't wait for the 30-day window.
Days 14–30
Formal winner assessment
Decide what to scale and cut. You're assessing the previous sprint's output while the current sprint is in production.
Weeks 5–10
Peak ROI window
Maximum payoff on confirmed winners. The return on creative investment made 4–6 weeks prior.
